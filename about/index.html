About

Enjoy the mind flow! 🎶

My name is Xiaxian Ou (欧夏娴). I am a biostatistics master student in Department of Biostatistics and Bioinformatics at Emory University, working under the supervision of Razieh Nabi on causal path-specific effect (PSE) analysis. My research focuses on the multiply robust estimators derived from influence functions and their applications in health disparities.

Before starting my master’s program, I earned my bachelor’s degree in Medicine from Peking University. Shifting my career from medicine to biostatistics is not an easy way, but I believe it is the right way. My experiences have given me the courage to never stop trying, and to pursue what I truly believe in.

Beyond my academic interests, I am also interested in psychology. Psychological concepts helps me better understand the cognition and regulate my emotions—it’s a cool and fascinating process!
